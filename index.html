Finding your Doshas

Ayurveda divides all living beings, not just humans, into three distinct body types based on their constitution. Each unique constitution is determined by what basic element is dominant in their bodies.

These unique body types are called Vata body type, Pitta body type, and Kapha body type depending on the element that is dominant in their body.
